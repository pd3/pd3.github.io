spVCF (version v0.5.0-0-g4b9e325)
pigz -dc $in | spvcf encode --threads 8 | bgzip -c --threads 8 > $out
pigz -dc $in | spvcf encode --squeeze --threads 8 | bgzip -c --threads 8 > $out
spVCF extends VCF by using a sparse encoding for the SITES x SAMPLES genotype matrix. Its lossless encoding is based on the expectation of identical QC values at nearby sites for a given sample. With the parameter --squeeze, the format is lossy. In this mode, it suppresses fields other than genotype GT and read depth DP from cells with zero alternate reference reads in AD, also DP is rounded down to a power of two in these cells.
